Income Tax Returns

An income tax return (ITR) is a form that taxpayers use to report their income and taxes paid to the government. It helps in calculating the liability of a person for income tax and also serves as proof of income and taxes paid. It is required to be filed if the income exceeds a certain threshold limit.

GST Returns

GST return is a document that businesses in India file with the government to report their GST-related transactions such as GST collected from customers and GST paid to suppliers. It is typically filed on a monthly or quarterly basis, depending on the turnover and other factors.

TDS Returns

TDS return is a form that shows how much taxes a company or employer has taken out of an employee's salary or payment they made to a vendor or contractor. It is a way for the government to collect taxes on a regular basis by deducting it at source and reporting it.

Financial Adviser

A financial advisor is a professional who helps people manage their money and make smart financial decisions. They provide advice on investments, taxes,retirement planning, and budgeting and other financial matters.


Company Registration

Company registration is the process of legally forming a company and obtaining a unique identification number by following the rules and regulations set by the Companies Act, 2013.

GST Registration

Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an indirect tax (or) consumption tax used in India on the supply of goods and services

Firm Registration

Firm registration is the process of making a company or partnership official by giving it legal recognition from the government, so it can do business and own property.

LLP Registration

LLP registration is the process of making a limited liability partnership official by getting government recognition, which separates the partnership from the partners and limits their legal responsibility.


PAN Card

A PAN card is a unique identification number issued by the Indian government to individuals and entities for financial transactions. It's mandatory for many financial activities and is required for opening bank accounts or filing income tax returns.

Project Report

A project report for loan process is a document that presents information about a business project to potential lenders. It provides details about the project, including its goals, market, financial projections, and how the loan will be used.

Accounting

Accounting is the process of recording and reporting financial transactions, it provides information for making business decisions. It's a way to keep track of a company or an individual's financial situation.

Book Keeping

Bookkeeping is the process of recording, classifying and summarizing financial transactions to provide information about a business's financial position. It's an essential part of accounting, and goal of bookkeeping is to ensure that financial records are accurate and up-to-date.

F.A.Q

  • What is the due date for filing the Income Tax return?

    In case of Company and persons other than company who is subject to audit, the due date shall be 30th October and in case of others it shall be 31st July.

  • What is the penalty for late filing of GSTR 3B?

    The penalty for late filing of GSTR 3B is Rs. 50 per day of delay. If the tax liability is NIL , the laet fee would be Rs. 20 per day.

  • Whether a registered person is required to file the GST return for the month in which no transaction are there?

    Yes. The registered person is required to file the returns every month or every quarter as the case may be, even If there are no transaction in the tax period.

  • What is the TDS form to be used for salaries?

    Form 24Q will be used to file TDS returns for salaries.

  • What is the TDS rate if the payee does not furnish his PAN to the payer?

    If the PAN is not furnished the TDS rate shall be 20%.
